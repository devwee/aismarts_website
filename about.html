A small team with a big vision for smarter decisions.

AI Smarts is an Australian AI startup focused on building tools that make complex decisions easier, more transparent, and more confident.

Our Story

People make big decisions every day — selling valuable assets, navigating relationships, choosing between lifestyle options — often with incomplete or unclear information.

The three founders of AI Smarts came together with a shared belief: that powerful, data-driven insights shouldn't be locked away in corporate boardrooms. They should be accessible to everyone, in the moments that matter most.

We started AI Smarts to bring AI-powered decision intelligence into everyday life — building tools that are practical, transparent, and designed for real humans.

Our Mission

To empower people with AI-driven insights so they can make smarter, more confident decisions in the moments that matter.

Our Vision

A world where anyone can access intelligent tools that turn complex, data-heavy questions into simple, actionable answers.

Our Approach to AI

Practical Impact

We build AI tools that solve real problems, not technology for its own sake. Every product must deliver tangible value.

Transparency

We're honest about what our AI can and cannot do. We clearly communicate limitations and uncertainties.

Human-Centered

AI should support human decisions, not replace them. We design tools that empower people, not automate them away.

Continuous Improvement

We iterate based on real-world feedback, continuously refining our models to be more accurate and useful.
